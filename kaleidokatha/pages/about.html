About Kaleidokatha

Preserving Kerala's Cultural Legacy for Future Generations

Our Mission

Kaleidokatha was born from a deep love and respect for Kerala's extraordinary cultural heritage. Our mission is to create immersive digital experiences that bring traditional art forms to life, making them accessible to a global audience while supporting local artists and cultural preservation efforts.

Our Story

How a passion for culture became a mission for preservation

2020 - The Idea

During the pandemic, when cultural performances were restricted, we realized the urgent need for digital platforms to showcase and preserve Kerala's art forms. The idea of Kaleidokatha was born.

2021 - Building the Team

We assembled a diverse team of cultural experts, technologists, artists, and storytellers who shared our vision of making Kerala's heritage accessible to the world.

2022 - Research & Development

Extensive research across all 14 districts of Kerala, documenting art forms, interviewing masters, and developing our interactive map platform.

2023 - Launch

Kaleidokatha goes live, offering an immersive journey through Kerala's cultural landscape to audiences worldwide.
