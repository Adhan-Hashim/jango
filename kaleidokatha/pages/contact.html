Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I contribute to preserving Kerala's cultural heritage?

There are several ways to contribute: share your knowledge of traditional art forms, connect us with master practitioners, volunteer for documentation projects, or support our initiatives through partnerships and sponsorships.

Can schools and educational institutions use your content?

Absolutely! We offer educational packages and resources for schools, colleges, and cultural institutions. Contact us to discuss custom educational programs and content licensing.

Do you offer guided cultural tours or experiences?

Yes, we partner with local cultural organizations to offer authentic experiences. These include artist workshops, cultural immersion programs, and guided tours to traditional performance venues.

How can artists showcase their work on your platform?

We're always looking to feature talented artists. Traditional performers, craftspeople, and cultural practitioners can apply through our artist partnership program. We provide platforms for showcasing work and connecting with audiences.

Is the platform available in Malayalam?

We're working on multi-language support including Malayalam, Hindi, and other regional languages. Currently, the platform is available in English with plans for localization in 2024.
